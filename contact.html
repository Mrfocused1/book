Common Queries

How long does the gel last?

+

Our fresh sea moss gel lasts up to 3 weeks in the refrigerator. You can also freeze it in an ice cube tray for up to 6 months without losing potency.

Where do you ship?

+

We currently ship nationwide across the United Kingdom. International shipping to Europe and North America is available for our Raw Dried Moss only.

Is it safe for children?

+

Yes, sea moss is a natural whole food. However, because it contains iodine, we recommend consulting your pediatrician before adding it to a child's diet.
